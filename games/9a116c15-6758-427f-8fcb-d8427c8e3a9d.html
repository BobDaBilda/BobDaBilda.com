Description

VTuber Editor is a professional VTuber avatar editing tool. Bring more creativity to your VRM avatar by adding paintings, VRM accessories, VTuber backgrounds, 3D animations, and more.

【VTuber Editor】Current Core Functions:

  • Avatar editor
    • Rich avatars, more than 30 public VTuber avatars, and support for uploading your own VRM model;
    • Painting tool, to paint your avatar model to make your avatar more personalized;
    • Rich 3d accessories, to decorate your avatars, and provide hand-held props such as gun, and microphone to enhance the live broadcast performance;
    • Advanced interactive props, which can interact with devices such as a mouse, keyboard, handle, etc.;
  • VTuber background editor
    • VTuber Background, providing 40+ exquisite dynamic and dynamic VTuber background resources, suitable for a variety of live broadcast scenarios;
    • Overlay, providing nearly 200 overlays to decorate your living room;
    • What's more, Vtuber Editor supports the resource upload function, all your artistic creations can be applied to your live room.
  • 3D Animation arrangement
    • Visual programming, real-time preview, and 3D animation can be quickly programmed by simply placing source blocks;
    • Built-in rich 3D avatar animations, expressions, special effects, and support for uploading text, pictures, special effects resources, free combination, empowering creativity.
  • Coming soon features
    • Avatar animation advanced editing;
    • Avatar one-click dress-up;
    • Customize avatar expressions;
    • Automated feedback for twitch chat;
    • ...
There are some other special features waiting for you to explore.

What can VTuber Editor do?

As an avatar editing tool, VTuber Editor provides professional solutions for VTuber avatar and VTuber background creation. You can create secondary creations for your avatar, including graffiti painting, ornament decoration, interactive prop loading, 3D animation arrangement, etc., and supports the creation of VTuber backgrounds.
All the works you create in VTuber Editor will be synchronized to VTuber Maker, and you can use them for live streaming and video creation through VTuber Maker.
