Description



Unleash your virtual potential with Warudo—the flexible and personalized 3D VTubing software you have always wanted.

  • 100% free for personal use.^
  • Webcam/iPhone ARKit face tracking and hand tracking included.
  • Full body tracking with mocap suits, VR trackers or any VMC-compatible software.
  • Compatible with VRM, VRChat, or any humanoid-rigged models. Non-VRM models require an one-time export in Unity (check out our handbook!).
  • Fully customizable expressions.
  • Add your own 3D props, environments, particles, etc.
  • Native integration with livestreaming platforms, e.g. Twitch, YouTube, Bilibili.
  • Let your audience interact with your character: throw items, spawn stickers & particles, launch water beams, etc.
  • Blueprint system that lets you customize anything—from tracking to interactions.
  • Control your blueprints with MIDI, Stream Deck, or even WebSocket messages.
  • C# SDK for developers to add features that you desire!
  • ...I don't want to make this a super long list. Explore the rest of our features on your own!

Please note that models used in the videos & images are NOT necessarily included in the release.

^ We define "personal use" as:
  • the copyrights of your VTubing persona are exclusively yours, and you possess ownership of your accounts on social/livestreaming platforms; and
  • there are no contractual obligations requiring you to stream for X hours per week, unless such a contract is directly in agreement with a major livestreaming platform (e.g., Twitch, YouTube).

If you would like to use Warudo commercially, please contact us at info@warudo.app.
