Description

BAPBAP is a roguelike party game where you craft the most unfair builds to make your friends hate you.

Invite and play with up to 7 friends in an all out brawl. Pick a random power after every round and craft the most ridiculous (or regrettable) build possible.

BAPBAP is best played with friends who can handle betrayal. Set up custom games with wild rulesets, or jump into community-run servers and embrace the chaos. No matchmaking. No ranks. Just pure, dumb fun.

Tight, free-flowing combat that’ll have you screaming for your mom to get the camera. Everything is dodgeable so if you get clapped, that’s on you.

Or a goldfish piloting a mech. Or a Russian samurai. Or ramen with legs. Each characters comes with it’s own unique skillset and there’s a character design for everyone—even the freaks 👁️👄👁️.

Whether it’s turning into a giant metallic duck, speccing into turretmancy, or literal poison, every game will present you with freshly randomized build combos to gamba with. Build synergies, get winnergies.

Block escape routes with a crane’s payload, transform into a vending machine for a sneaky ambush, or do a drive-by in a 1942 Panzerkampfwagon Tiger Tank. BAPBAP is a PvP sandbox where nothing make sense but also everything makes sense.

On top of all that, you could get ganked by a roaming giga slime, fish up a nuclear bomb or come face to tentacle with [redacted]  - the better you are at adapting, the more you’ll win.

Whether it’s the turnaround 1v3, hitting a sweet multi-target knock up or just watching your mates get hit by their own trap – BAPBAP is here to help you get that sweet hit of dopamine in this increasingly depressing world.

Welcome to BAPBAP, where logic goes to die.
