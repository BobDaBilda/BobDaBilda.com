Description


You can start managing your market in the Prologue version of 'Bazaar Simulator' and enjoy the limited experience. Don't forget to add and follow the full version of the game on your wishlist.

Traditional Scale System

Weigh your fruits and vegetables accurately using the traditional scales used in markets.

Stock Ordering and Management

Purchase your stocks, organize their placement, and transfer them from crates to stalls. Remember, fruits and vegetables start to spoil over time.

Interactive Sales Experience

Assist customers in their selections and manage payments both in cash and via QR code or IBAN. Ensure every customer leaves satisfied, creating repeat customers and a lively market atmosphere.

Adapt to Market Fluctuations

Buy products when prices are low and set competitive sales prices in the dynamic market environment. Grow your business by balancing profit margins with customer satisfaction.

Invest and Expand

As your profits increase, invest in your market. Expand your market, upgrade your stalls, and improve the shopping environment.

From Lemon Stand to Market Empire

In "Bazaar Simulator", every choice affects your success. Will you build your market empire starting from a lemon stand, or will you remain a lemon seller?
