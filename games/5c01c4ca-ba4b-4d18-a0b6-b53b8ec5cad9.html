Description

Are you looking to upgrade your aiming skills? Then you need Aimlabs!


Aimlabs is a training platform designed by gamers to help sharpen up your core FPS aiming skills. Aimlabs offers a comprehensive set of tools to improve your aim, regardless of which FPS you play. It provides curated aim training scenarios that will turn you into an aim God in no time.

Dive into specifically designed aim training exercises to master the art of flicking, tracking, speed, perception, and cognition. What sets Aimlabs apart is its ability to provide detailed aim analysis feedback, giving you valuable insights and guiding you towards rapid improvement.

Aimlabs is continuously updated with new features to ensure compatibility with a wide range of games. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned pro, Aimlabs is an invaluable tool for gaining a competitive edge.



Reasons to train:


  • Over 29,000 training tasks & playlists
  • New - Ranked Seasons, a new way to track your progression and compete against others in Aimlabs. Each season features new tasks to measure and improve your aiming skill. Climb the leaderboards, and earn in-game rewards while setting your eyes on becoming the world’s best aimer!
  • Detailed aim analysis with improvement tips
  • Create unlimited maps, tasks & scenarios in Creator Studio
  • 1:1 recreations of VALORANT maps
  • Official VALORANT VCT training partner
  • Official Rainbow Six Siege development partner
  • Over 500 FPS game profiles with a sensitivity convertor to match your settings including VALORANT, Apex Legends, CS:GO, R6S, Call of Duty & more.
  • Test your skills on our leaderboards against other players & dive into our special events!
  • Track your improvements over time with your personalized dashboard
  • Great for warming up before hitting ranked!

Train your way:


  • Customize your training; targets, crosshair, sounds & more
  • Controller & keyboard / mouse support
  • 20+ weapons to train with or create your own!
  • Custom Recoil Patterns
