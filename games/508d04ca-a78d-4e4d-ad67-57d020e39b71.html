Description

Museum Mystery is a fusion between deckbuilding rogulite game and narrative adventure with intriguing secrets waiting for you to uncover.



Your first night as a museum security guard takes a disastrous turn.
Animated exhibits, awakened gods, and total chaos. And it’s your job to restore order until dawn.

Explore Various Eras

Museum halls are portals into history. Literally. Walk through each era, from Ancient Egypt to the 19th century, to find the roots of what’s happening.

Battle With Unique Enemies

Museum exhibits have come to life. And most of them are mad. Try to calm down this amazing pieces of art.

Master Hundreds of Cards

Find cards of ancient artifacts or practices. Combine them, unlock new mechanics and craft your perfect hand.

Adventure Through History and Art

Talk with historical figures, complete side-quests and help legendary characters!

Features

  • 4 historical eras to explore
  • 150+ cards to create the best deck possible
  • Unique relics that give unprecedented power
