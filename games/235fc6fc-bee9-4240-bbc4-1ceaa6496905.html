Description

A chaotic local platform fighter where even the worst player can win.

In a world turned into a massive junkyard after humans disappeared, vermin have taken over. Rats, seagulls and other pests clash in an absurd monarchy for one thing only: to sit on the throne!

Royal Vermin is a 2 to 4 player local fighting game inspired by the best platform fighters, featuring ranged combat, chargeable projectiles and constantly unbalanced arenas. Every match is fast, unpredictable and always different.

Online play is not officially supported.
However, you can play remotely using Steam Remote Play Together.
At least 2 players are required to play.

KEY FEATURES

  • 2 TO 4 PLAYER LOCAL GAME: Perfect for game nights with friends and same-screen play.

  • SIMPLE AND ACCESSIBLE CONTROLS: Easy to pick up, even for new players.

  • UNIQUE RANGED COMBAT: Grab projectiles, charge your shots and knock your opponents out of the level.

  • EVOLVING ARENAS: Levels collapse during combat and become increasingly dangerous.

  • REVENGE MODE: Even when eliminated, you can influence the match and cause spectacular comebacks.

  • HIGH REPLAYABILITY: Dynamic levels and special stages that break up the pace with varied objectives.

Free to play, Royal Vermin is designed to keep every match tight until the end and make you want to play just one more round.
