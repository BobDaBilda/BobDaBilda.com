Description

Another time, another life... this is your story.


It's time to get up.
You have to leave the country in the next 24 hours for some important business. Still feeling on a different planet after last night, right? Was it a big party?
Well, too bad: you realize you have forgotten crucial documents at work. You really need all this so, drive there, hoping that there is still someone to let you enter. If not this will be an illegal entrance.
Your head hurts? Be brave, it will only take two minutes and you'll be back home for diner.

What have you done?


Forbidden Trip is a free Total Conversion for Amnesia: The Dark Descent. In this horror-adventure story you will experience events going from the unusual to the bizarre, in original, modern environments.
The beginning of your voyage? Home.
Why is all this happening? Find by yourself… be careful.
Things can go beyond logic… stay focused.

You cannot really tell what but something feels out of place.
Maybe your bad drug habits plus all those drinks have an effect on that, still.
This time it's different. No clear memories of what happened last night. A bad feeling.

Let's go for a walk.

Features:

  • Original environments in an alternate, contemporary Japanese city.
  • Inspired by Asian horror stories, legends & games (Corpse Party, Silent Hill, Project Zero, Nanashi No Game…).
  • Old-school or modern graphics, your choice. Psychedelic places… obligatory.
  • Curiosity kills the cat… sometimes. Different (wrong) endings.
  • Original soundtrack, including tracks composed by Arcane Strain, Cosmonecromantum, Zoulgow and others!
  • Unique hint system, never get stuck… or get fooled? Who is sending you those emails, those links anyway?
  • Some ARG elements & secrets inside or outside of the main game… will you find them all?

Send your feedback or ask for help in the forums. Have fun! :)
