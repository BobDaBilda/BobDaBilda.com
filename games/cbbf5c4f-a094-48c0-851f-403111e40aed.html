Description

Gator Hucker is a fast-paced brawler where YOU craft your own unique playstyle!

You are alien alligators with wacky abilities locked in battle, either in teams or a chaotic free-for-all! 

Huck yourself into the arena with the help of cybernetically enhanced gorillas and cause chaos for glory and entertainment!

Two Ways to Play!

Engage in either 4 vs 4 team deathmatch or up to eight player free-for-all action!

Navigate and explore an intricate gladiatorial arena.

Utilize Abilities

Swap between your Bipedal and Quadrupedal forms and use their unique attributes to your advantage.

Two forms means twice the abilities! Try out all three special abilities for both the Bipedal and Quadrupedal forms for a new gameplay experience every time.

Unleash Devastating Ultimate Abilities

After enough damage has been dealt, it's time to unleash your Ultimate Attack! Choose between the Super Laser or the Gorilla Strike to deal massive amounts of damage to your opponents!

Huck, Fight Win!

Upon being defeated immediately rejoin the action by getting Hucked back into the arena by cybernetically enhanced gorillas! Land on opponents to deal incredible damage or land further away from the action, it's your choice!
