Description

Brand-New Season “Lunaria” Launches April 16!

Anniversary Event: Midas Touch

The 3rd Anniversary Celebration arrives together with the new Season events! By logging in daily and completing event quests, Hunters can claim abundant rewards, including a Legendary Drop Pactspirit Selection Pack, combat Pactspirit draws, Exclusive Weapon Appearances, and more. In addition, the Midas Touch event invites Hunters to run maps for a chance to receive a commemorative gold note, created in a special collaboration between Torchlight and the China Academy of Art.

Season-Exclusive Gameplay: Lunaria

Beneath the radiant moonlight of the Netherrealm, petrified Lunar Stone Statues await the Hunters’ awakening. Capture more statues to trigger massive chain explosions, then challenge awakened Lunari monsters for increasingly valuable rewards. Immerse yourself in the smooth, satisfying rhythm of Lunaria and enjoy farming at its purest!

New Hero: Scent Weaver · Sage

The Scent Weaver · Sage makes her debut with the first-ever Trait, “Licorice Note.” Built around the unique rhythm of Elixirs and Blending, she fluidly shifts between sustained buffs, agile offense, and all-new mechanics—bringing Hunters a refreshing and exhilarating combat experience!

New Progression System: Memory Revival

Introducing Memory Revival, the new core progression system! With special items, Hunters can awaken memories to greatly boost existing affix values and unlock rare Affixes. Reforge your builds and unleash even more possibilities!

Art & System Upgrades

The UI, hero skill effects, scene visuals, and login music have all received a full refresh. Alongside these audiovisual upgrades, key systems such as the Trade House, search functions, Hardcore Mode, injury buffer feedback, and function lock have also been optimized—delivering a smoother, more immersive map-running experience for Hunters!

FAST & THRILLING Battles

With no stamina and no cooldowns, smash down incoming waves with melee attacks, blast off magical explosions and draining pools, or snipe down ranged enemies. Grind with your own battle style!

Collect ENDLESS Loot

Unlimited drops from battles to upgrade build styles and establish your own collection. Prove your grind power by showing off in the in-game free market.

Build UNLIMITED Playstyles

With unique heroes, 24 talent tabs, 200+ legendary gears, 240+ powerful skills, Try out infinite playstyles or strategic possibilities on hero builds. Build your very own hero!

Trade AT WILL

Utilize the Trade house to be a part of a booming economy with an infinite amount of hero builds to trade. One hunter's trash might be another hunter's treasure!
